Design is a broad term, so a logo for Denver Design Week must be fluid to be inclusive of designers from all backgrounds. I created a logo system that could be adapted depending on which field of design it was being used for. The general theme draws imagery from shapes in logos from previous years, as well as some mountanous textures to incorporate Denver's skyline. I wanted to emulate puzzle pieces that can join together and represent all the different fields of design that come together to make this event special.
