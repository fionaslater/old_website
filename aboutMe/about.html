Hi I'm Fiona. I am a Technology, Arts, and Media Design major at the University of Colorado Boulder. I have a passion to create and design through painting, digital art, and drawing. I love to take mundane objects and turn them into something extraordinary that tells a story. I focus on pushing myself to break through what is “normal” and strive to find a different way to see the world around me. I am passionate about working with those who do good and seek to influence positve change with my work. My inspiration for creating art orginiates from the places and people I love, and I am excited to see more beautiful places and meet more remarkable people.
